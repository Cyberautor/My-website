COMPANY OVERVIEW

TKR was established in 2013 after discovering the high rate of poor academic performance and learner drop-out on an annual basis.
The organisation involves young people from different universities and unemployed teachers who are committed
in making a difference in the education sector by assisting learners through one-on-one tutoring.

Our aim is to provide learner with first-hand learning experience, taking into consideration their abilities
thus far and continue to assist the learners based on what they could not understand during their regular school session.

Background.

Thuto Keya Rona tutors was started in 2015, the organization provides after-school lessons,
access to government information and relating to unemployment, health, arts and culture, Bana Pele principles.

The after-school program was established for the purpose of creating high quality after school programs outside of regular school hours.
After-school programs are defined as programs that take place when school is not in session and provide educational enrichments
and recreational activities for students in grade R – 12 and have a parent involvement component.
After-school programs provide students with academic enrichment opportunities, as well as additional activities designed to
complement the Department of Basic Education’s academics programs. Services includes tutoring and mentoring, homework help,
academic programs (such as hands on science or technology programs), community service opportunities, as well as music,
arts, sport, and cultural activities. At the same time, programs assist working parents by providing a safe environment for students.

The specific purpose of the grants is to implement or expand programs outside of regular school hours that offer students
both academics/ educational and enrichment activities, such as youth development activities,
drug, violence and pregnancy prevention programs, counselling, projects based learning, art music, technology education programs,
service learning, character education and recreation programs that are designed to reinforce and complement the regular academic program
of participating students, as well as provide parent involvement opportunities for families.

Projects Background.

After-school project

Our after-school sessions offer a range of services including academic tutoring, psycho-social support, career guidance, and lots more. Volunteers work in small groups (ratio 1:10) and our learners drive the agenda by bringing work they are struggling with to the tutoring sessions. The small groups enable vital individual attention and fast feedback. It is through this learner-centric approach that the learners can be able to fill the gaps in their knowledge and strengthen their grasp on fundamental conceptual basics.
